[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The shadows fall across the wooden floor
I've never wanted anyone like this before
Come closer now, forget the outside storm
Just pull me in and hold my body warm

[Verse 1]
The firelight is dancing on your skin
A quiet night where losing means we win
No ticking clocks to rush the way we feel
Just steady breaths and something deeply real

[Pre-Chorus]
I trace the lines along your open hand
We found a place where only we can stand
The midnight air is slipping through the room
Let’s leave behind the shadows and the gloom

[Chorus]
So close the door and leave behind the storm
I'll trade the whole wide world for what we share
Just run your fingers slowly through my hair
And stay right here to hold my body warm

[Post-Chorus]
Hold my body warm, right through the night
Keep me right here until the morning light

[Verse 2]
Your fingertips are tracing down my spine
A gentle touch that tells me you are mine
I feel the quiet rhythm of your chest
A perfect place for weary souls to rest

[Pre-Chorus 2]
I trace the lines along your open hand
We found a place where only we can stand
The midnight air is slipping through the room
Let’s leave behind the shadows and the gloom

[Chorus]
So close the door and leave behind the storm
I'll trade the whole wide world for what we share
Just run your fingers slowly through my hair
And stay right here to hold my body warm

[Post-Chorus 2]
Hold my body warm, right through the night
Keep me right here until the morning light

[Bridge]
The embers slowly fade into the gray
But what we have will never wash away
If you ever shiver, Saeed will be your spark
I'll keep you safely glowing in the dark

[Spoken]
(I just want to memorize the rhythm of your breath)
(Come closer to me, let the rest of it fade away)

[Final Chorus]
So close the door and leave behind the storm
I'll trade the whole wide world for what we share
Just run your fingers slowly through my hair
And stay right here to hold my body warm
